About House Maxify

House Maxify is a modern, data-driven portal for people buying or selling homes. We combine an advanced machine learning model with a clean, approachable interface so anyone can get a quick, useful price signal — plus comparable homes that actually feel comparable.

What Makes It Good

  • Focused, honest estimates with a sensible range
  • Inputs in plain language, not data science jargon
  • Comps filtered by zipcode and key features
  • Fast prototype that’s easy to extend or deploy

Technology

Flask XGBoost pandas scikit-learn joblib Docker (optional)

Trained on cleaned King County housing data; the app infers model features, handles medians for missing values, and stores leads for follow-up.

How It Works

  1. Submit property details (beds, baths, sqft, grade, zipcode, etc.).
  2. The model predicts a price; we calibrate results to dollars.
  3. We show a price range and 3 close comps in your area.

Roadmap
